Treatments

Everyone’s pain threshold is completely different. The sensation is similar to plucking multiple eyebrows simultaneously or a cat’s scratch. Majority of clients expect the discomfort to be a lot worse. We apply a local anaesthetic before procedure to minimise discomfort.
The duration depends on several factors, including skin type, aftercare and technique. Results vary from person to person. Typically, they start fading within a year or two, but some may last up to longer with proper care.
The treatment will take around one hour and a half to two hours, including consultation, pre draw and initial treatment. Once treatment is completed, the healing process lasts up to 2 weeks, ready for your 4 week top up.
